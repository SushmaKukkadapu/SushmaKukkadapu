Spoken English - For Engineers and Scientists


About the Author:

Dr.Sinha is a rocket scientist, an inventor and educator. He received his Ph.D. in engineering from University of Minnesota, USA. He has 30 years of experience in academia and high-tech industries. He has contributed to many successful international space projects and his work has appeared in Guinness World Records 2013. He travels around the globe teaching systems engineering, innovative design, and English for technical communications to thousands of students and teachers.


Salient Features:

  • 20 Lessons encompassing all major science and engineering branches. Science: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology, and the current scientific research. Engineering: Mechanical, Electrical, Civil, Chemical, Electronics, Computer Science, Mechatronics & Robotics, Interdisciplinary Engineering, and recent developments in technology.
  • The essential vocabulary of 8000 technical terms with correct pronunciation.
  • 3000 most useful technical sentences including explanations and applications in a clear voice using international-standard English.
  • 200 Paragraphs for improving listening comprehension and fluency.
  • 50 interesting topics for brainstorming, innovation, and projects.
  • Two special lessons designed to help students prepare for job interviews, group discussions, meetings and technical presentations.
